# Code of Conduct

## Our Commitment
We want this project to be a welcoming and respectful space for everyone.

## Expected Behavior
- Be respectful and constructive in communication.
- Assume good intent and provide helpful feedback.
- Focus on the project’s goals and collaborative problem-solving.

## Unacceptable Behavior
- Harassment, discrimination, or personal attacks.
- Trolling, insulting comments, or disruptive behavior.
- Sharing private information without permission.

## Reporting Issues
If you experience or witness unacceptable behavior, please open a private report with project maintainers through the repository’s contact or support channel.

## Enforcement
Maintainers may remove, edit, or reject contributions and interactions that violate this Code of Conduct.
